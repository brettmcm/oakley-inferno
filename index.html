PRIZM™ INFERNO™

          

        

        
        

          

            
STAY FOGLESS

          

          

            
Think turbocharged window defroster – in your goggles! Inferno heated lens technology is the latest in the innovation you’ve come to expect from Oakley.

          

        


      

    







  

    

      

        

          
          
          
        

      

    

    


            

        

          
1 Button Activation

          
Combat inner lens fog with the push of a button.

        

      

            

        

          
3 Minute Heating Cycle

          
Even heating across the lens causes fog to evaporate, and leaves it nowhere to hide.

        

      

            

        

          
All Day Comfort

          
Through rigorous field testing we determined the ideal lens temperature to combat fog, without ever feeling uncomfortably warm on your face and eyes.

        

      

      
    

  









  

  

    

      
    

    

      

        
POWER MODULE

        
CHARGED UP

        
An external power module keeps the goggle lightweight so it fits and feels like a traditional Oakley snow goggle. It is designed to be completely removable when fog is not an issue. The rechargeable and replaceable battery is engineered for excellent performance in cold weather.

      

    

  


  

    

      
    

    

      

        
LINE MINER

        
NO COMPROMISE

        
Inferno™ heated lens technology is available only in the new Line Miner goggle, with Prizm™ Lens Technology. Prizm™ enhances visibility of snow contours, bumps and textures so you can ride with confidence.

        
      

    

  







  

    
    
INFERNO™ TECHNOLOGY

    
HOW IT WORKS

    
Simple and intuitive 2-button design. One to power on the unit for the day and one to activate each Inferno™ heating cycle of 3 minutes. The heating cycle will run its course and turn off automatically.

  


  


    

      
      
      
      
Press To Activate

      
Heating Cycle Activated

      

        
        
      

    


    
TECH SPECS


    

    
          

        

        
HAPTIC VIBRATION

        
A slight vibration acknowledges the heating cycle has started and ended

      

          

        

        
HEATING CYCLE

        
  • Heating Cycle: 3 min
  • Active Battery Life: 25-30 Inferno™ cycles
  • Defog Time: Under 2 min from fully fogged

      

          

        

        
LED Indicator

        
  • Solid green: at least 25% battery life
  • Solid red: less than 25% battery life
  • Blinking green: Inferno mode active
  • While charging: blinking red indicates in progress, solid green indicates full charge

      

          

        

        
Lightweight

        
Power module weight: 100 grams, 3.5 ounces (comparable to a deck of playing cards)

      

          

        

        
Lithium Ion Battery

        
Excellent cold weather performance (stable to -20°C or -4°F) compared to other consumer electronic batteries commonly used in your phone or camera which can stop working when the temperature drops below freezing

      

          

        

        
USB Charging

        
Plug into any USB charging outlet, whether a wall charger, car charger, computer, or USB battery pack
